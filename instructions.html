Instructions

Use the left and right arrow keys to wander around. Collect powerups like wall-jump or double jump and most importantly collect all the ornaments and go to the campfire. It sounds easy, huh? But you should also avoid all the enemies. Be careful! They are everywhere and if they touch you, you lose one life. If your lives go down to zero, then you lose and the game begins again.
